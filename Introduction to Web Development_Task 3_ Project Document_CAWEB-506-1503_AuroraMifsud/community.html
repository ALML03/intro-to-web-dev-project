Frequently Asked Questions

How do I start a new discussion?

To start a new discussion, log in to your account, navigate to the relevant forum category, and click on the "Start New Discussion" button. Fill in the title, description, and select any relevant tags before submitting.

Are there community guidelines I should follow?

Yes, we have community guidelines to ensure a respectful and supportive environment. These include being constructive with feedback, avoiding personal attacks, respecting intellectual property, and keeping discussions relevant to the topics.

How can I get featured in the community spotlight?

Community spotlights are selected based on active participation, helpful contributions, and quality of work shared. Consistently engaging in discussions, providing valuable feedback, and showcasing innovative work increases your chances of being featured.

How long does it take to get a response from the admin team?

Our admin team typically responds to inquiries within 24-48 hours during business days. For urgent matters, please indicate this in the subject line of your message for faster attention.
