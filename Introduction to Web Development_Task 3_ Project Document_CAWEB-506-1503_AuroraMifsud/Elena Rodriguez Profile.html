About Elena

Born and raised in San Francisco, Elena Rodriguez discovered her passion for digital art while studying Fine Arts at the California College of the Arts. After graduating with honors in 2020, she began her career as a freelance digital artist, quickly gaining recognition for her distinctive style that blends fantasy elements with vibrant color palettes.

Elena draws inspiration from her Mexican heritage, incorporating traditional folklore and mythology into her contemporary digital creations. Her work has been featured in several digital art publications, and she has collaborated with game development studios to create character designs and concept art.

When not creating art, Elena teaches digital illustration workshops at local community centers and mentors young artists. She believes in the power of art to connect people across cultural boundaries and tell stories that resonate on a universal level.

"I aim to create worlds that feel both fantastical and familiar," says Elena. "My art is about finding the magic in everyday life and bringing it to the surface through vibrant colors and dream-like imagery."
