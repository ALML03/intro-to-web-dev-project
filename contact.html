Frequently Asked Questions

Creating an account on ArtSpire is simple! Click on the "Sign Up" button in the top-right corner of the page. Fill out the registration form with your name, email address, and create a password. You can also sign up using your Google or Facebook account for faster registration. Once you've completed the form, check your email for a verification link to activate your account.

To upload your artwork, log in to your account and navigate to the "Upload" page from the main menu. Click on the "Upload Artwork" button, then drag and drop your image files or use the file browser to select them. You can upload images in JPG, PNG, or TIFF formats up to 20MB per file. Add a title, description, tags, and categories for your artwork to help others discover it. Once you've filled in all the details, click "Submit" to publish your artwork.

Yes! ArtSpire is completely free to use. You can upload your artwork, create a profile, and engage with the community at no cost. There are no Premium or Pro plans. Everyone has access to the same features as we focus on building an inclusive creative hub for all artists.

For now, ArtSpire is a creative hub designed to showcase your artwork to the world. While selling artwork directly through the platform isn’t available just yet, the team is actively working on introducing a marketplace feature. Stay tuned, as selling capabilities are coming soon!

ArtSpire takes copyright protection seriously. When you upload artwork, we automatically apply a visible watermark to prevent unauthorized use. Premium and Pro users can customize their watermarks or choose to display their work without visible watermarks. All images on ArtSpire are also protected with right-click disable and embedded metadata. If you discover unauthorized use of your artwork, you can report it through our Copyright Infringement form, and our team will take appropriate action.
